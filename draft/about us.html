About the Company

Radio Systems Professionals

A communication product and service company.

Established in May 2007, RSPRO Enterprises is engaged in the distribution of high availability products and associated services for leading businesses. We specialize in delivering wireless technology products and uninterruptible power systems matched with a full complement of services. Our business practice is highly customer oriented and values customer satisfaction and timely delivery at the highest regard.

RSPRO Enterprises is the Philippine distributor of Commscope-Andrew wireless products, Meru Wireless Network products, single phase and 3-phase APC-Schneider Electric UPS products and EATON UPS products, UPS replacement batteries and services. Performed various field trials, pilot installations, proof of concept and retrofits for Commscope-Andrew base station products such as repeaters, base station antennas, filters, RF cables, microwave antennas, in-building solutions (DAS) and accessories to local carriers. Performed specification compliance test for various cable and base station antenna systems and accessories for local carriers. Facility maintenance contractor for the Metro Rail Transit Line 3 (MRT 3) including on-board train communications, repeater systems, portable radios and accessories. Maintenance contractor for various data center UPS systems.
